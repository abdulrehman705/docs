## Choose Your Integration Path

Bluum supports two ways to embed investing, depending on your product.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Self-Directed Investing" icon="chart-line" href="/get-started/quick-start-self-directed">
    Let your users buy and sell stocks, ETFs, and fractional shares. You control the UI — Bluum handles accounts, compliance, custody, and order execution.

    **Best for:** Trading apps, invest tabs, stock gifting, round-ups.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wealth Management" icon="briefcase" href="/get-started/quick-start-wealth">
    Offer managed portfolios, financial planning, risk assessments, and automated investing. Full wealth lifecycle through the API.

    **Best for:** Robo-advisors, financial planner apps, employee benefits.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a dashboard account">
    Sign up at [dashboard.bluumfinance.com](https://dashboard.bluumfinance.com/login) to get your sandbox API credentials. No approval required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first call">
    Test your credentials by listing tradable assets from the sandbox environment.
    ```bash
    curl -X GET 'https://test-service.bluumfinance.com/v1/assets?asset_class=us_equity&tradable=true' \
      -H 'Authorization: Basic '$(echo -n 'YOUR_API_KEY:YOUR_API_SECRET' | base64)
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the Quick Start">
    Walk through account creation, KYC, bank linking, and your first trade in under 30 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Complete the [production readiness checklist](/get-started/go-live) and request production credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>
