The Buildmarkets Wealth Management API lets you embed a full suite of automated investing features directly into your product. Instead of your users picking individual stocks, Buildmarkets' strategy engine manages portfolios on their behalf — driven by risk tolerance, financial goals, and investment policies you configure.

## What Wealth Management includes

Wealth Management builds on the same account, funding, and trading foundation as self-directed investing, and adds a dedicated layer of financial planning and portfolio automation:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Investor Profiles" icon="user">
    Capture a comprehensive financial picture for each user — demographics, employment, tax situation, insurance, estate planning, and investment preferences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Risk Assessments" icon="shield-check">
    Run questionnaire-based risk tolerance scoring that drives portfolio construction. Map user answers to risk bands and let Buildmarkets select the appropriate strategy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Goals & Financial Plans" icon="bullseye">
    Define financial objectives (retirement, home purchase, education) with target amounts and timelines. Buildmarkets generates a financial plan that maps each goal to an investment strategy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Managed Portfolios" icon="chart-pie">
    Create portfolios governed by an Investment Policy Statement. Buildmarkets handles rebalancing, drift monitoring, and trade execution automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-Invest" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Schedule recurring contributions that automatically invest into a portfolio — daily, weekly, or monthly — without any manual intervention from your users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advised Accounts" icon="briefcase">
    Open accounts with `management_type: "advised"` so the strategy engine can place trades on behalf of end users within the bounds of their IPS.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Typical use cases

Wealth Management is the right integration model if you're building any of the following:

- **Robo-advisors** — automated, goal-based investing with minimal user input
- **Financial planner apps** — tools that map users' financial goals to investment strategies
- **Employee benefits platforms** — managed investment options as part of a workplace benefits package
- **Round-up and micro-invest products** — automated portfolio contributions triggered by spending or recurring schedules

## How it differs from self-directed

| | Self-Directed | Wealth Management |
|---|---|---|
| **Account type** | `management_type: "self_directed"` | `management_type: "advised"` |
| **Who places trades** | End user through your UI | Buildmarkets' strategy engine |
| **Additional APIs** | None | Profiles, risk, goals, financial plans, portfolios, auto-invest |
| **Time to first trade** | ~30 min in sandbox | ~1 hour in sandbox |

Both models use the same account onboarding, KYC, bank linking, and funding flows. Wealth Management adds the advisory layer on top.
