After an order fills, a position appears in the investor's account. This guide explains how to fetch positions, interpret every P&L field, build a portfolio view in your UI, and keep that view up to date as market prices change.

## Listing positions

Fetch all current holdings for an investor with a single request:

```bash
curl -X GET "$BASE_URL/investors/$INVESTOR_ID/positions" \
  -H "Authorization: Basic $AUTH"
```

## Position detail

Fetch a single position by its ID:

```bash
curl -X GET "$BASE_URL/investors/$INVESTOR_ID/positions/$POSITION_ID" \
  -H "Authorization: Basic $AUTH"
```

## Understanding P&L fields

Each position includes four fields that together tell the full profit-and-loss story:

| Field | Formula | What it tells you |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| `total_cost_basis` | `quantity × average_cost_basis` | What the investor originally paid for these shares |
| `market_value` | `quantity × current_price` | What those shares are worth right now |
| `unrealized_pl` | `market_value − total_cost_basis` | Dollar gain or loss on paper |
| `unrealized_pl_percent` | `unrealized_pl ÷ total_cost_basis` | Percentage gain or loss on paper |

<Note>
  **Unrealized** means the gain or loss exists on paper — the investor hasn't sold yet. Once they sell, the P&L becomes **realized**. Only unrealized P&L is available from the positions endpoint.
</Note>

## Displaying positions in your UI

Use these patterns to present portfolio data clearly:

### Portfolio summary

Aggregate across all positions to show the investor's overall portfolio health:

- **Total market value** — Sum of all `market_value` fields
- **Total unrealized P&L** — Sum of all `unrealized_pl` fields
- **Overall percentage return** — `total_unrealized_pl ÷ total_cost_basis`

### Per-position row

Show each position as a row in a holdings table or list:

- Symbol and company name
- Quantity held (supports fractional shares like `5.42`)
- Current price (`current_price`)
- Total market value (`market_value`)
- Unrealized P&L — display in green for positive values, red for negative
- Percentage change (`unrealized_pl_percent`)

### Real-time updates

Positions update as market prices change throughout the trading day. Choose the strategy that fits your product's needs:

- **Polling** — Query `GET /positions` every 30 seconds during market hours. Simple to implement and works well for lower-frequency updates.
- **Webhooks** — Subscribe to `order.filled` events to detect when new positions are created or existing ones change size. Combine with a price feed for live `current_price` and `market_value` updates.

## When positions update

| Event | Effect on positions |
|-------|-------------------|
| Buy order fills | A new position is created, or an existing position's `quantity` and `average_cost_basis` increase |
| Sell order fills | Position `quantity` decreases; the position is removed entirely if fully sold |
| Market price change | `current_price`, `market_value`, `unrealized_pl`, and `unrealized_pl_percent` are recalculated |
| Stock split | `quantity` and `average_cost_basis` are adjusted proportionally — `total_cost_basis` and `market_value` remain accurate |

## Empty positions

<Note>
  When an investor sells their entire holding in an asset, the position is removed from the list entirely. A `GET /positions` response only includes assets the investor currently holds — a 200 response with an empty array `[]` means the investor has no open positions.
</Note>
