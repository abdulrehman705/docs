The Bluum platform gives you a complete set of APIs for embedding investment experiences directly into your product. Every concept below is a core building block you'll use throughout your integration — from onboarding a user and verifying their identity, to placing trades and managing their portfolio.

## Core Concepts

These pages cover the fundamental primitives every Bluum integration depends on.

  <Card title="Authentication" icon="lock" href="/platform/authentication">
    Authenticate with HTTP Basic Auth using your API Key and Secret. Learn how to encode credentials, set required headers, and manage keys safely across environments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accounts" icon="user" href="/platform/accounts">
    Investment accounts are the root object connecting wallets, orders, and positions. Learn about account types, lifecycle statuses, required fields, and management modes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance & KYC" icon="shield-check" href="/platform/compliance-kyc">
    Every account must complete Know Your Customer verification before trading. Learn how to upload identity documents, track verification status, and handle rejections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wallets" icon="wallet" href="/platform/wallets">
    Wallets hold cash balances for investment accounts. Understand wallet types, how money flows, holds, balance fields, and the transaction ledger.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Funding & Transfers" icon="building-columns" href="/platform/funding">
    Move money between a user's bank account and their wallet via ACH or manual bank transfer. Learn the Plaid Link flow, deposit lifecycle, and idempotency requirements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trading" icon="chart-line" href="/platform/trading">
    Place buy and sell orders, work with fractional shares, and understand order lifecycle, types, and market hours.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
